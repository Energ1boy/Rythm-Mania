Privacy Policy for Rhythm Mania

Effective Date: 23/07/2024

Welcome to Rhythm Mania! This privacy policy outlines how we handle information when you visit our website, rythmmania.vercel.app. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about the data we collect and how it is used.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal data directly. However, we use Vercel Analytics to collect non-personal data about your interactions with our website. This data includes:

  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our website, such as pages visited and actions taken.
  • Technical Data: Information about your device and browser, such as IP address and browser type.

2. How We Use Your Information

The non-personal data collected by Vercel Analytics is used to:

  • Improve our website based on usage insights.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and other tracking technologies to enable Vercel Analytics. These cookies help us gather information on site usage and improve user experience. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect site functionality.

4. Third-Party Services

Our website uses Vercel Analytics to monitor and analyze site performance and user interactions. Vercel Analytics collects non-personal data and helps us understand how our website is used. For more details on how Vercel Analytics handles data, please review their Privacy Policy.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect the data collected through Vercel Analytics. However, no online system can guarantee absolute security, and we cannot ensure complete protection against unauthorized access.

6. User Rights

As we do not collect personal data directly, there are no specific rights related to data access, correction, or deletion applicable to your personal information. If you have questions about the data collected by Vercel Analytics, please contact them directly.
